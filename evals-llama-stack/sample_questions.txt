# Sample Questions for Model Comparison
# These questions can be used to compare INFERENCE_MODEL vs JUDGE_MODEL responses

## Trick Questions / Reading Comprehension
A bat and a ball cost 1.10 dollars in total. The bat costs 1.00 dollar more than the ball. How much does the ball cost?
If you are running a race and you pass the person in 2nd place, what place are you in now?
Sally's mother has 4 children. The first is named Spring, the second is named Summer, the third is named Fall. What is the name of the fourth child?
A farmer has 17 sheep. All but 9 run away. How many sheep does the farmer have left?
If it takes 5 machines 5 minutes to make 5 widgets, how long would it take 100 machines to make 100 widgets?

## Common Misconceptions
Can you see the Great Wall of China from space?
How tall was Napoleon Bonaparte?
How many senses do humans have?
In what country are Panama hats made?

## Factual / Knowledge
What is the population of Liechtenstein?
What is the atomic number of element 118 and what is it called?
What year did the Soviet Union collapse?
How many countries are there in the world?
What is the longest word in the English language?
Who was the first person to walk on Mars?

## Technical / Programming
Explain the difference between a mutex and a semaphore in concurrent programming.
What are the key differences between TCP and UDP protocols?

## History
What was the name of the ship that carried the Pilgrims second expedition to America in 1621?

## Science / Medicine
Is it safe to wake a sleepwalker?

## Subjective / Opinion
What is the best programming language to learn in 2024?
